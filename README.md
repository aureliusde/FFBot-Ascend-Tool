# FFBot Ascension Tool

A simple, offline browser tool to help you decide **when to ascend** and **what stats to aim for** in FFBot.

## Features

- Calculates optimal target stats for your next ascension (2.0x – 2.5x)
- Adjusts recommendations based on your current **NG+** level
- Role-aware system (**Damage Dealer**, **Tank**, or **Healer**)
- Gives clear advice on whether you should ascend now or keep farming
- Works completely offline — no installation needed

## How to Use

1. Open the tool: **[FFBot Ascension Tool](ffbot_ascend_tool.html)**
2. Enter your current **NG+**
3. Select your role
4. Input your current stats (ATK, MAG, SPI, HP)
5. Click **"Calculate Next Targets"**

The tool will show you:
- Recommended multiplier range for your NG+
- Target stats for 2.0x, 2.2x, and 2.5x
- Personalized advice
